Volunteering

Volunteering at the library as tech help meant being able to troubleshoot diverse issues. The variety kept things interesting and pushed me to think on my feet. I also ran small workshops where we broke down everyday tools into simple steps, creating a space where people could ask questions without pressure. What I enjoyed most was seeing how small bits of troubleshooting could open doors for people and make technology feel more approachable.

Collaborative design project focused on creating a net-zero energy multifamily housing prototype. Developed using Rhino and Grasshopper. Integrated passive design strategies, renewable energy systems, and performance simulations to optimize sustainability, occupant comfort, and efficiency while looking to meet Solar Decathlon competition criteria.

File System Simulator

This project is a command-line simulation environment that mimics a simplified Unix-like system. It allows users to navigate directories, manipulate files, and run basic commands directly from the terminal, without requiring a real Unix setup. The goal was to create an educational tool for beginners to experiment safely with Unix commands, build muscle memory, and understand how a command-line environment works.
