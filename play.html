Volunteering

Volunteering at the library as tech help meant being able to troubleshoot diverse issues. The variety kept things interesting and pushed me to think on my feet. I also ran small workshops where we broke down everyday tools into simple steps, creating a space where people could ask questions without pressure. What I enjoyed most was seeing how small bits of troubleshooting could open doors for people and make technology feel more approachable.
